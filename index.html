Our Journey

2018

Sonderful is founded by Sarah Mitchell and Dr. Marcus Chen with a vision to transform learning through immersive theatre.

2019

Launch of our first corporate program with five Fortune 500 clients, establishing our methodology.

2020

Pivoted to virtual immersive experiences during the pandemic, reaching clients globally.

2021

Launched our university program, partnering with 25 institutions across North America.

2023
